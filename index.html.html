About GigBroz

Built for the Niche. Trusted by the Industry.

GigBroz was founded by cloud security practitioners who were frustrated by generic freelance platforms that couldn't understand the depth of security and compliance work. We built a specialized marketplace where expertise actually gets matched with complexity.

Today, GigBroz serves startups, mid-market companies, and enterprises across healthcare, fintech, SaaS, and government — connecting them with credentialed experts in cloud security, audit, GRC, and DevSecOps.
